About Training Monitor App

Built for EMS educators. Backed by MedTech. Free forever.

Every EMS program deserves great tools, not just the well-funded ones.

At MedTech, we’ve spent years working directly with EMS educators and students across the country. One thing we’ve heard again and again: “We’d love to use a training monitor in our classroom, but we just can’t afford one.”

Real defibrillators and training monitors are incredible, but they’re also expensive. Many programs are running on shoestring budgets, doing everything they can to stretch every dollar. And yet, the pressure to deliver high-quality, hands-on training has never been higher.

That’s why we created the Training Monitor App.

Why We're Giving It Away

We believe cost should never be the reason a student gets less out of their education.

We built this tool because we care about students passing, about instructors having what they need, and about EMS programs being empowered, not held back.

The App is:

  • ✅ Free Forever: No subscriptions, no licenses, no catch
  • ✅ Supported by MedTech: Funded entirely by us
  • ✅ Improving Constantly: Because you deserve the best
  • ✅ Accessible: Works on nearly any device with an internet connection

Whether you're using it in the classroom, on a projector, or in small group simulations, the Training Monitor App gives you the flexibility of a remote-controlled, simulated cardiac monitor, without the $10,000 price tag or yearly subscription.

A Note From LJ, Founder of MedTech

“I started MedTech because I remember what it felt like to be a student with limited access to free resources, and I’ve seen how hard instructors work to provide quality training. This app is my way of giving back, to level the playing field and help raise the bar for EMS education everywhere. It’ll always be free. And we’ll always keep making it better. My only ask of you is simple: Please share it!”
